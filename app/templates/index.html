Why you should trust Skyner

Powered by LEOCET< /h4>

LEOCET is our trajectory calculating tool. It uses the incredibly precise MIT-developed module Skyfield to run the calculations in the reliable python language.

Efficient and Precise

We use the best tools avalaible in various libraries to produce accurate results without overtaxing the computation abilities

Around the clock monitoring

We use real-time data to determine the present and future position of the debris. This allows us to monitor the trajectories of the debris in order to estimate the risk of collisions.

Client Support

Our staff is always ready to answer any questions you may have and guide you through the use of our platform if you need it.

Skyner is developed as part of the first year student project at CentraleSupélec. This project is the initiative of the CEA, which wishes to study possible solutions to be deployed to fight the difficulties raised by the increasing number of debris orbiting the Earth.
